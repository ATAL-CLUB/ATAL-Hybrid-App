The university provides a free shuttle bus service for students and staff between Ealing Broadway station and our Brentford site.

The service operates a circular route, stopping at:

• Brentford site Paragon

• Little Ealing Lane - for Student Village housing in South Ealing

• South Ealing underground station

• Warwick Road (UWL Main Campus)

• Bond Street (Bus Stop N)

• Ealing Broadway Centre (Bus Stop L) 

• Haven Green (Bus Stop D) - for Ealing Broadway station and Villiers House 

At Brentford, there is an on-campus bus stop located at Paragon House for the shuttle service.

It is required that you have a university ID to access the shuttle bus service.

Visitors to the University of West London should carry a letter of authorisation or a visitors pass to be able to use the service.
